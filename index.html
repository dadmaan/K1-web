Who is Keivan

I have been an EFL (English as a Foreign Language) teacher and language consultant for 15 years, and the holder of CELTA (Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults) from Cambridge University. For the past ten years, I have also been teaching group and private IELTS courses for those who are planning to study or live abroad. So far, I have conducted several courses including Business English, Commercial Correspondence and essay writing.

I have studied English Literature and languages have always been by biggest ambition all these years. Therefore, I also teach German and I am familiar with other European languages as well. Reading and traveling have been two of my biggest passions in life, for which knowing languages are essential.
