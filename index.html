Who is Keivan

I have been an EFL (English as a Foreign Language) teacher and language consultant for 17 years, and the holder of CELTA (Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults) from Cambridge University. For the past ten years, I have also been teaching group and private IELTS courses for those who are planning to study or live abroad. So far, I have conducted several courses including Business English, Commercial Correspondence and essay writing.

I have studied English Literature and languages have always been my biggest ambition all these years. Therefore, I also teach German and I am familiar with other European languages as well. Reading and traveling have been two of my biggest passions in life, for which knowing languages are essential.

My Services

I have mainly concentrated on holding private sessions and workshops for IELTS candidates to make the best out of their own knowledge and helping german students who wish to study in German speaking countries to meet their educational or occupational requirements.

Also, discussion classes and courtroom debates have always been a shining aspect of my career as you can have the chance to speak freely and without stress.

IELTS

I have been teaching IELTS, both academic and general modules, useful for those who are going to sit the exam during a limited time. Both test-taking techniques and time management points are covered in my courses.

German

I have been teaching german for some years and during this period, I have concentrated on A1 and A2 exam preparation for those who intend to study in Germany, Austria and Switzerland.

Online Courses

Online courses, including free discussion and IELTS speaking interviews are also my strong points for those who either do not have the time or have distance problems all over the world.

Business English

I am also active in the field of business interpretation and translation, for those businesspeople who do not have a strong command in speaking English.
